Removing notarization credentials

Once you have entered credentials for notarization, they are stored in your account’s login keychain. If you wish to invalidate these credentials later, use the following procedure:

 

1. Go to the  Script2Pkg  menu and select  Settings…

 

 

2. Select the  Credentials  option.

3. Click the  Invalidate Credentials  button.

 

4. Click the  Invalidate  button in the confirmation dialog.

Once the credentials have been invalidated, the  Notarize packages  option will no longer be enabled in the main app window.
